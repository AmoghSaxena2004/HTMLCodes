NINJA HATTORI



This is about Ninja Hattori!





11-year-old Kenichi Mitsuba is an average kid who goes to secondary school and struggles with his studies, he is very stubborn and is very lazy and therefore always ends up frustrating his parents and teacher. He loves to find an easy way of things much to the annoyance of Hattori.

Meanwhile, a little ninja named Hattori Kanzo becomes best friends with Kenichi. Hattori is now a part of the Mitsuba family along with his brother Shinzo, and his ninja dog, Shishimaru. Hattori helps Kenichi with his problems, and constantly keeps an eye on him, as a good friend. Yumeko is portrayed as Kenichi's love interest. The main antagonist Amaara, a Koga Ninja and his ninja cat Kio. Amaara always troubles Kenichi. Kenichi asks Hattori to take revenge as a recurring storyline in many episodes. Although Hattori is a good friend, Kenichi sometimes fights with Hattori due to misunderstandings created by Amaara. Sometimes Jippou, Togejirou and Tsubame help him.



Characters

  • Ninja Hattori

    • He is 11 years old and 40 kg and is 140 centimeters tall. He is the boy who usually contests Amaara/Kemumaki when he performs mischief. Hattori's main weakness is that he is afraid of frogs!

  • Kenichi Mitsuba

    • Is a 10-year-old boy who goes to middle school and is poor in his studies. He likes Yumeko, however also Amaara/Kemumaki is always after her. He also tricks Hattori to help him in any situation, even in extreme ones!
    Link to Kenichi's Image

  • Shinzō

    • Shinzo is Hattori's younger brother. He uses weapons made from wood. He is a ninja-in-training who learns to be a good ninja, like Hattori. He wears red coloured ninja robes.
    Link to Shinzo's Image

  • Shishimaru

    • Is a ninja dog living with Kenichi, who came along with Hattori and Shinzo. He is a casual behaving dog who at times becomes lazy and stubborn. Along with Shinzo, the two of them wind up doing mischief and creating trouble.
    Link to Shishimaru's Image

  • Amaara/Kemumaki

    • Is the antagonist of the show. He, along with his cat Kio/Kagechiyo, always are the ones who cause trouble. Strength-wise, he has competition with Hattori and Shinzo. He is 11 years old and is shorter than Kenichi but a lot more fitter and stronger than him.
    Link to Amaara/Kemumaki's Image

  • Kio/Kagechiyo

    • Is a talking animal-ninja of the Kōga Ninja clan, Kio/Kagechiyo is the helping antagonist in the series. Usually Amaara/Kemumaki gives a big task in his plans for Kio/Kagechiyo to carry out, which he often does not succeed in. This is because he doesn't get enough training from Amaara/Kemumaki and is seen sleeping on the streets.
    Link to Kio/Kagechiyo's Image

  • Yumeko

    • A.k.a. Yumeko-chan, is the girl who is sincere, She likes Amaara/Kemumaki and Kenichi, who usually causes disputes between Amaara/Kemumaki and Kenichi. She also attends the same middle school as the two.
